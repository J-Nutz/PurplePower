Welcome To JNuts' Mod Page for Purple Power Purple Power is a mod in development. It's main concept is solar panels, purple ore, purple tools, and more to come! Feel free to leave suggestions!

Feel free to look through my GitHub repo!

Here's how you download

Goto the folder that you want the Purple Power folder in
Shift right click (Windows) chose open command window here and enter this

$ <C:\users\your_name\your_folder> git clone https://github.com/J-Nutz/PurplePower.git

Wait for it to download, then point your IDE to that path!
